About the Game

SHEEP_HOME is an immersive farming simulation game where you start as a sheep on a farm. As you progress through levels by completing farming tasks, you unlock new features and abilities. At level 10, you can transform into a human for an enhanced farming experience!

Game Features

First-Person View

Experience farming like never before in immersive first-person perspective.

Progression System

Level up by completing farming tasks and unlock new abilities.

Human Transformation

Transform into a human at level 10 for enhanced capabilities.

Power-ups

Collect and use special power-ups to boost your farming efficiency.

Interactive Environment

Interact with crops, animals, and various farm objects.

User Accounts

Save your progress and unlock features with your account.

Power-ups

Power-ups are special items that give players temporary advantages or abilities in the game. They can only be used when you are in human form and are unlocked at level 15.

FF (Fast Forward)

Speeds up time, making crops grow faster and animals produce more quickly.

Double Harvest

Doubles the yield from crops and animals for a limited time.

Killer Weed Killer

Instantly removes all weeds from the farm.

Pest Control

Eliminates pests that may be damaging crops.

Auto Harvester

Allows automatic harvesting of crops.

Super Speed

Increases the player's movement speed.
